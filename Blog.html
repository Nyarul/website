Sustainability Tips

Prioritize quality over quantity, support eco-friendly companies, and rent or buy used items for special events. Reduce the number of times you wash your clothing, use eco-friendly detergents, let them air dry, and learn how to do basic repairs. Increase their lifespan by upcycling or trading, and properly get rid of worn-out things by recycling or donating them.




Fashion Tips

To create adaptable ensembles, mix and match your clothing, experiment with layering, and invest in classic items. Make mindful accessory choices, wear what gives you confidence, and pick colors and designs that go well with your personality.




Trends Overview

Follow style blogs, influencers, and fashion weeks to stay abreast of major trends. Bold colors, standout pieces, eco-friendly textiles, and a blend of classic and contemporary designs are common elements of current trends.




Lifestyle Insights

Prioritize self-care, personal development, and thoughtful wardrobe choices to adopt a balanced lifestyle. To improve both style and well-being, include eco-friendly practices, pursue imaginative pastimes, and prioritize wellbeing.




How to Style Vintage Clothing

To get a well-balanced style, combine classic items with contemporary essentials. Emphasize statement pieces, like as a dress or jacket from the past, add modern accents, and adjust the fit for a professional look.




The Benefits of Capsule Wardrobes

By encouraging conscientious consumption, saving time, and lessening choice fatigue, capsule wardrobes make your life easier. In order to reduce waste and promote sustainability, they advise investing in high-quality, adaptable components that can be combined and rearranged.




Top Fashion Influencers to Follow

  • Chiara Ferragni: An Italian entrepreneur and fashion blogger known for her blog 'The Blonde Salad,' Chiara has a massive following and collaborates with major fashion brands.
  • Dixie D’Amelio: An American singer and social media personality who gained fame on TikTok, Dixie has ventured into fashion, influencing millions with her style.
  • Olivia Palermo: An American socialite, fashion influencer, and entrepreneur, Olivia is renowned for her impeccable style and has been a front-row fixture at fashion shows worldwide.
